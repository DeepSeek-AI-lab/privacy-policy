2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Data Collected by the Extension

Our Chrome extension collects and processes the following types of data:

Locally Stored Data (Chrome Storage API)

  • User Preferences: Settings and configuration options you choose within the extension (e.g., sidebar position, theme preferences, default AI model selection)
  • Extension State: Information about the extension's operational state (e.g., sidebar open/closed status)
  • Authentication Tokens: Session tokens if you sign in to access premium features

Storage Location: This data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API and is NOT transmitted to our servers.

Display Information (System Display Permission)

  • Screen Dimensions: Your screen width and height are accessed solely to calculate the optimal size and position for popup windows

Usage: This information is used only for window positioning and is NOT stored or transmitted.

URL Collection and Usage Data

  • Page URLs: URLs of web pages where you activate or use the extension sidebar
  • Session Data: Timestamps when the extension is used
  • Feature Usage: Which extension features you use

Purpose: This URL data helps us understand which types of websites users find the extension most useful on, allowing us to optimize for those use cases.

2.2 Data Processed by DeepSeek AI

When you interact with the AI chat interface embedded in this extension, your data is processed by DeepSeek AI according to their privacy policy:

  • Chat Messages: All text inputs, prompts, and questions you submit to the AI chat
  • Uploaded Files: Any files, images, or documents you share with the AI (if applicable)
  • Chat History: Your conversation history with the AI assistant
  • Account Information: If you create a DeepSeek account (email, username, password, date of birth)
  • Usage Data: IP address, device identifiers, browser type, operating system, language settings
  • Behavioral Data: Interaction patterns and usage analytics
Important: Chat interactions are processed through DeepSeek AI's servers. Please review DeepSeek's Privacy Policy for complete details on how they handle your data.

2.3 Data Processed by Aitopia.ai (Technology Partner)

Aitopia.ai provides the underlying technology platform for this extension. As our technology partner, they may process:

  • Extension Usage Data: URLs where you use the extension, feature usage patterns, session timestamps
  • Technical Data: Device information, browser type, IP address
  • User Content: Data you share through the extension interface may be processed to improve the service
Important: Aitopia.ai's data practices are governed by their privacy policy. Please review Aitopia's Privacy Policy for complete details on how they handle data.

2.4 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT track your browsing history or websites you visit
  • We do NOT read the content of web pages you visit
  • We do NOT collect personally identifiable information beyond what you provide to DeepSeek
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT collect data when the extension is not actively in use

  • To provide the AI sidebar interface on web pages

  • To position popup windows correctly on your screen
  • To maintain your session state across browser sessions

3.2 Service Delivery

  • To connect you to DeepSeek AI's chat service

3.3 Analytics and Improvement

  • To analyze which types of websites users find the extension most useful on
  • To identify popular features and areas for improvement
  • To measure extension usage and engagement
  • To optimize user experience based on URL patterns

4. Data Storage and Location

4.1 Local Storage (Your Browser)

User preferences and settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

4.2 DeepSeek AI Servers

AI chat data (messages, files, conversation history) is transmitted to and stored on DeepSeek AI's servers. Data handling is governed by DeepSeek's privacy policy.

4.3 Third-Party Services

The extension may communicate with the following domains:

  • chat.deepseek.com: DeepSeek AI chat service
  • *.aitopia.ai/*: Extension backend services
  • *.chatgptextension.ai/*: Support services

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

5.1 DeepSeek AI

When you use the AI chat functionality, your chat data is shared with DeepSeek AI. For information about how DeepSeek processes and shares your data, please see: DeepSeek Privacy Policy

5.2 Analytics Partners

URL data and usage analytics may be shared with analytics service providers to help us improve the extension. This data is anonymized and does not include personally identifiable information.

5.3 Extension Developer

We (the extension developer) do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

6. Chrome Extension Permissions Explained

Our extension requests the following permissions:

6.1 Storage Permission

Why we need it: To save your preferences, settings, and extension state locally in your browser.

What we do: Store user preferences (sidebar position, theme, default model) locally.

What we DON'T do: Upload this data to external servers or share it with third parties.

6.2 System Display Permission

Why we need it: To get your screen dimensions for proper popup window positioning.

What we do: Calculate window size and position to center popups on your screen.

What we DON'T do: Store or transmit your screen information.

6.3 Content Script Injection (All URLs)

Why we need it: To provide the AI sidebar interface on any web page you choose to activate it.

What we do: Inject the sidebar interface when you click the extension icon or activate it.

What we DON'T do: Read or collect data from web pages without your explicit action. The sidebar only loads when you activate it.

6.4 External Connectivity

Allowed domains:

  • *.aitopia.ai/* - Extension support services
  • *.chatgptextension.ai/* - Additional extension features

Purpose: To enable communication between the extension and necessary backend services.
