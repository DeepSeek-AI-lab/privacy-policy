Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the "DeepSeek AI Sidebar" browser extension ("Extension", "we", "our", or "us") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By installing and using this Extension, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Single Purpose

This Extension provides quick access to the DeepSeek AI chat service directly from your browser toolbar. Users can open the DeepSeek chat window with a single click and use the AITOPIA AI sidebar on any webpage.

Information We Collect

User Preferences (Stored Locally Only)

We store the following preferences locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Popup Display Settings: Whether to show the popup window when clicking the extension icon.
  • Privacy Consent Status: A flag indicating whether you have explicitly consented to the sidebar accessing page content.

Purpose: To remember your settings across browser sessions, provide a personalized experience, and ensure we do not access page content without your permission.

Storage Location: Locally on your device via chrome.storage.local or chrome.storage.sync. This data may sync across your Chrome browsers if you are signed into Chrome sync, but is never sent to our servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be absolutely clear about what we do not collect:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Health information
  • Financial or payment information
  • Authentication credentials (passwords, PINs, security questions)
  • Personal communications (emails, texts, chat messages)
  • Location data (GPS, IP address, region)
  • User activity data (clicks, mouse movements, keystrokes) associated with your identity
  • AI conversation content or chat history (these remain between you and the AI provider)

Anonymous Usage Data

We may collect limited anonymous usage data strictly for product improvement purposes. This data:

  • Does not include personally identifiable information
  • Cannot be used to identify you
  • Is used only to understand general feature usage and improve performance

We do not sell, rent, trade, or monetize this data in any way.

Limited Data Transmission

We do not transmit any personal data, page content, or chat history to external servers.

As noted above, limited anonymous usage data may be collected for product improvement purposes. This data:

  • Does not contain personal information
  • Is not linked to your identity
  • Is used only to improve functionality and user experience

We do not sell, share, or transfer this data to third parties.
No tracking pixels or invasive analytics tools are used.
No third-party analytics services are integrated.
All core Extension functionality operates entirely within your browser.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Saving Your Preferences: Remembering your display settings and consent choice.
  • Providing Functionality: Injecting the AITOPIA sidebar on webpages you visit (only after you grant permission).
  • Product Improvement: Analyzing anonymous usage data to enhance the Extension's performance.

Data Sharing

We Do Not Share Any Data

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer any information to third parties. Period.

Third-Party AI Services

When you use this Extension to access DeepSeek or AITOPIA:

  • Your interactions with DeepSeek are governed by DeepSeek's Privacy Policy
  • Your interactions with AITOPIA are governed by AITOPIA's Privacy Policy

This Extension acts as a convenience tool to access these services. We do not intercept, store, or transmit your conversations with these AI services.

Permissions Explained

This Extension requires certain browser permissions to function:

Permission Why It's Needed
storage To save your preferences (popup settings and consent status) locally on your device.
system.display To center popup windows on your screen for a better user experience.
host_permissions (<all_urls>) To enable the AITOPIA AI sidebar on the webpages you visit.

Important Note: The <all_urls> permission is required to act as a sidebar on every page. We access page content solely to inject and render the sidebar interface on the pages you visit. This activity occurs only after you have explicitly granted consent via our in-page banner. We do not transmit this page content to our servers.

Your Rights and Control

Clear Your Data

You can clear all Extension data at any time:

  1. Go to chrome://extensions/
  2. Find "DeepSeek AI Sidebar"
  3. Click "Details"
  4. Click "Clear data" under "Site access" (or "Extension options")

Uninstall

Uninstalling the Extension removes all locally stored preferences.

Children's Privacy

This Extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.
